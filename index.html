Japan packing list

This is a packing list for your Japan Exchange Year

  • Your home country's Cookbook
  • Photo Album or mini scrapbook
  • Postcard from home
  • A small national flag patch or pin
  • Local snacks or sweets
  • A journal or travel diary
  • Gift set for Host Family
  • a comfort item from home

  • Polaroid camera or mini photo printer
